I'm a part of the visualization team at Databricks in Seattle, WA. Prior to Databricks, I was a member of the research team at Tableau. My research is at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction and Information Visualization. Specifically, drawing upon techniques from multimodal and mixed-initiative user interfaces, I design expressive and intelligent visualization tools for human-data interaction.
