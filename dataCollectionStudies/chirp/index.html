Example 1

The data table below shows fictional list of students at a university. The table contains details including student names, courses, student's year in the degree program, major, and the weekly number of study hours.

Possible questions you might ask include:

  • What are similarities between classes for Databases and Finite Automata?
  • Is there a preference for certain courses among students in different school years?
  • Who are similar students based on study hours?
  • Show the relationship between majors and courses.
  • Do students from some majors spend similar weekly study hours?
  • What are commonalities between Jon Snow and Tony Parker?

Example 2

The two data tables below show fictional data about employees at a software company. The attributes include the employee names, their roles within individual departments, their salaries. For each department, you have information about the city and state the department belongs to.

Employee Table

Department Table

  • Which roles have similar salaries?
